Installation

Follow these steps to get Salorid Client running on your machine.

  1. Download the Salorid_Launcher.exe from your dashboard.
  2. Run the installer as Administrator.
  3. Log in using your account credentials.
  4. Select your desired Minecraft version and click Launch.
